Event Information

All Campus Praise Night is one night where Christians from all over CMU
come together to praise the one true living God of the Bible.
This semester, the event will be held on September 5, 8:30 PM @ Porter Hall 100.

Theme

When I look at your heavens, the work of your fingers the moon and the stars, which you have set in place, what is man that you are mindful of him, and the son of man that you care for him?"
(Psalm 8:3-4 ESV)


Our theme for this Praise Night is GREATER. He not only created this whole universe, but also loved us insignificant beings so much that He offered up Jesus as a sacrifice so that we may live eternally. We often forget how colossial God is and how inconsequential we are. We want to remind Christians that we can live boldly for Christ because this mighty God is for us.


Volunteering

Praise

Have a heart for worship and a talent for singing or an instrument? We need musicians and singers. There will be at least three practices, spread across the week before Praise Night

Tech

Tech includes logistics and tech support. These are the unheard and unseen servants in almost every event. Logistics involves moving instruments and equipment to and from the venue. Tech support involves making slides, operating the laptop, and manning the mixer. You must commit to arriving early and leaving later to help with set-up and tear-down.

Hospitality

Hospitality includes outreach. We need a team of volunteers to welcome attendees, hand out name tags, and usher attendees during the event. Have a heart for following up? We also need a team to follow up with attendees.
