Welcome to the Unofficial ACF Summer Bible Study hub (2014 Edition). All info and links will be posted to this page, so please bookmark this page. It'll make your life easier. I promise!

For this summer, we will be studying the letters of John, namely 1, 2 and 3 John. The Epistles of John are a collection of letters believed to be written by John the Evangelist. The first letter was written as a sermon to help them understand why such a divine God would come live a mortal life and die an agonizing death. The second and third letters are targetted letters written to the "elect lady" and Gaius, respectively.

This series will be a ten-week study. The schedule is posted under the Schedule tab. We will be communicating via Google Hangouts. Hope to see you guys there!
